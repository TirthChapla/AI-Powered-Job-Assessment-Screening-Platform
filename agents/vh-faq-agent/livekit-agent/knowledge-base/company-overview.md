# VoiceHire Company Overview

## What is VoiceHire?

VoiceHire is an AI-powered recruitment platform that reimagines the hiring process through intelligent automation. We combine the power of a comprehensive Applicant Tracking System (ATS) with revolutionary AI agents to deliver a complete, unbiased, and efficient hiring solution.

## Core Product Philosophy

VoiceHire transforms recruitment through a dual-agent approach:

- **Recruitment Agent**: Handles job posting, candidate sourcing, screening, and workflow automation
- **Interview Agent**: Conducts comprehensive interviews, provides detailed assessments, and ensures bias-free evaluation
