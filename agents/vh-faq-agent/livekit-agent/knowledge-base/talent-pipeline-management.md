# VoiceHire Talent Pipeline & Application Management

## Talent Pool Management System

### Strategic Talent Organization

- **Custom talent pool creation** with names, descriptions, and visual color-coding system
- **Color-coded organization** with 9 predefined colors (blue, green, purple, red, cyan, orange, lime, pink, gray) for easy visual identification
- **Active/inactive status management** for pool lifecycle and organization
- **Pool statistics dashboard** showing total pools, total candidates, active pools, and average pool size
- **Permission-based access control** with granular create, read, and update permissions
- **Sortable pool listings** with candidate counts, status, and date tracking

### Advanced Candidate Assignment

- **Bulk candidate assignment** to multiple talent pools simultaneously
- **Dedicated candidate search interface** for efficient pool building
- **Notes functionality** for candidate assignment context and tracking
- **Pool membership display** on candidate profiles with visual indicators
- **Individual candidate removal** from pools with confirmation dialogs
- **Cross-pool candidate synchronization** for maintaining accurate relationships

### Business Benefits

- **Strategic talent pipeline development** for proactive recruitment planning
- **Improved candidate organization** through visual and categorical grouping
- **Long-term talent relationship management** for future opportunities
- **Reduced time-to-fill** through pre-qualified candidate pools
- **Enhanced candidate engagement** through organized relationship tracking

## Application Lifecycle Management

### Comprehensive Application Processing

- **Application listing with advanced filtering** by job, stage, UTM source, and text search
- **Multi-criteria sorting** by candidate name, job title, company, stage, applied date, score, and rating
- **UTM source tracking** for application attribution and recruitment channel analysis
- **Application creation forms** with job selection, candidate validation, and document upload
- **Automatic candidate deduplication** to prevent duplicate profiles and maintain data integrity
- **Permission-based application management** with role-controlled access to features

### Document Management & Resume Processing

- **Three resume viewing modes**: All Content, PDF View, and Parsed Content for flexible review
- **Intelligent resume parsing** with structured extraction of contact info, summary, experience, education, and skills
- **PDF viewer integration** with download capabilities for document review
- **Resume upload and replacement** functionality with file validation (PDF, DOC, DOCX, max 2MB)
- **Cover letter management** with editing capabilities and text processing
- **Specialized resume sections** including certifications, projects, publications, and awards

### Evaluation & Assessment Framework

- **Three evaluation categories**: Stage, Interview, and Additional evaluations for comprehensive assessment
- **Custom evaluation forms** with dynamic form selection and submission
- **Evaluation history tracking** with chronological organization and status management
- **Team collaboration features** for shared candidate reviews and feedback
- **Permission-based evaluation creation** ensuring appropriate access controls
- **Visual evaluation indicators** with status badges and progress tracking

### Follow-up & Feedback Collection

- **Follow-up request management** with custom form selection for targeted information gathering
- **Status tracking system** (Pending, Completed, Expired) with visual indicators
- **Request expiration functionality** for time-sensitive feedback collection
- **Response viewing and management** with structured data presentation
- **Comment system** for request context and team communication
- **Professional candidate communication** maintaining brand consistency
